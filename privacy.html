Privacy Policy for Score Sensei

Effective Date: February 24, 2026 | Last Updated: February 24, 2026

Joe Vander Veen ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Score Sensei mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Round Data: Disc golf scores, course information, disc selections, and shot details you record
  • Form Analysis Videos: Videos you record or upload for throw analysis
  • Voice Recordings: Audio when using voice input for round recording (processed and discarded after transcription)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, session duration
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the app experiences an error

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Generate AI-powered insights, stories, and feedback about your rounds
  • Analyze your throwing form using computer vision
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Send important notifications about your account or the App
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the App

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely on Firebase/Google Cloud Platform servers
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Video files are stored securely and can be deleted at your request
  • We retain your data as long as your account is active

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Deletion

  • You can request access to your personal data
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Contact us at scoresenseifeedback@gmail.com for data requests

Account Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings in the App
  2. Select "Delete Account"
  3. Confirm deletion

This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.

Device Permissions

Camera

  • Used only when you choose to record form analysis videos
  • Videos are uploaded for AI processing and deleted within 24 hours
  • You can revoke this permission in your device settings at any time

Microphone

  • Used only when you actively use voice input for round recording
  • Audio is converted to text in real-time; raw audio is not stored on our servers
  • You can revoke this permission in your device settings at any time

Speech Recognition

  • Used to convert your spoken round descriptions to text
  • Processing occurs on-device and/or via Apple's speech recognition services
  • Only active when you tap the microphone button to record
  • No audio recordings are stored after transcription
